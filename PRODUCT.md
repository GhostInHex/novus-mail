# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Operators, founders, chiefs of staff, and fast-moving teams who work from Gmail and Google Calendar all day. They are usually triaging a live queue, protecting calendar time, and trying to make quick decisions without losing control of what gets sent or scheduled.

## Product Purpose
NovusMail turns Gmail and Calendar into one command-driven workspace so users can triage priority threads, inspect meeting context, draft responses, and coordinate schedule changes from a single surface. Success means less tab switching, faster decision-making, and explicit approval before any irreversible action leaves the app.

## Brand Personality
Focused, fresh, controlled. The product should feel modern and immediate without becoming noisy, and intelligent without feeling automatic or reckless. The emotional goal is confident momentum: users should feel sharper, calmer, and more in charge of the queue.

## Anti-references
Do not make the product feel like a generic AI SaaS dashboard, a sterile enterprise admin panel, or a playful consumer inbox. Avoid decorative AI tropes, vague productivity language, and any flow that hides sends or calendar mutations behind automation theater.

## Design Principles
- Keep control visible at the moment of action.
- Compress the workflow, not the user's judgment.
- Make freshness feel operational, not ornamental.
- Let the interface read like a command deck, not a mailbox.
- Earn trust through clarity, confirmation, and legible state.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard accessibility across the core product surfaces. Preserve clear focus states, support reduced motion preferences, and keep operational copy plain enough for fast scanning under pressure.
